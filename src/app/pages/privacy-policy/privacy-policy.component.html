Privacy Policy

At UrbanNest, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This page explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it secure.

What We Collect

  • Account details such as name, email, and encrypted password.
  • Property listing details you submit, including photos and contact information.
  • Basic usage data required to improve search and listing experiences.

How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your UrbanNest account.
  • To publish property listings and help users connect with owners.
  • To prevent misuse, improve service quality, and maintain platform security.

Data Security

We use secure storage and access controls to protect your data. Passwords are stored in encrypted (hashed) format and are never saved in plain text.

Your Choices

You can request updates or removal of your account data by contacting support@urbannest.in. We process such requests in accordance with applicable laws.
